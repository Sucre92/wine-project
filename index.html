  • Bloemfontein Beer and Wine Fest is coming...

  • Yes, It's the summer festival!

  • 1st - 2nd October 2019

Hot off the press! Our 2019 Bloemfontein Beer and Wine Festival is scheduled for October 1st & 2nd.
Mark your calendars and watch this site for details.


WARM FUN-FILLED WEEKEND at the Park with tents, benches & tables, sponsors umbrellas, soft live music, a food court, brand activations in form of vehicle dealerships, tobacco companies, etc and numerous stalls for BEER & WINE TASTING and enjoyment. Expected number of about 2,000 patrons spread over weekend and 500 per day during the Beer Garden week.

PURPOSE

  • An event to add the product-mix i.e MACUFE.
  • To create business opportunities for locals.
  • Generate interest for patrons to prolong their stay in our city.
  • Create more spending avenues for patrons.
  • Platform for local up-and-coming artists.
  • A catalyst in the beverage and lifestyle events designed to stimulate activities in the local tourism industry.
  • A platform for local and regional micro craft brewers.
  • A permanent fixture on Mangaung social calendar.
